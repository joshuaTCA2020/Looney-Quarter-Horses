  • About Us

Lee and Brenda Looney started showing horses together upon their marriage in 2001. After taking time off from showing due
family obligations including raising their son Jay Looney, they decided to form Looney Quarter Horses, LLC in 2010 with the
goal of breeding top quality western pleasure and hunseat horses with futurities in mind. Through the years they have raised
World Champion Western Pleasure horses as well as many top ten horses in the nation. Located on the outskirts of Jackson, TN,
visitors are always welcome to stop by and see the horses that are available for sale.
